Preamble

The item(s) to be supplied under these Terms and Conditions are thereinafter referred to as civil works, equipments and services.

The supplier of civil works, equipments and services as defined in this proposal is SolidWurth Corp. thereinafter referred to as SolidWurth.

The buyer of these civil works, equipment and services is thereinafter referred to as Client.

The agreement comes into force when SolidWurth receives an Order Confirmation (i.e. Purchase Order or Contract) that has been signed by Customer.

1. Payment Terms
Payment schedule shall be agreed upon per scope of work as specified in the proposal. Payments are accepted through cash, check, or bank transactions.
2. Mobilization
Mobilization will commence ONLY upon receipt of downpayment, signed contract, NOA (Notice of Award) and NTP (Notice to Proceed)/purchase order.
3. Project Completion
Based on the specified items and their quantities specified in this document, SolidWurth commits to the delivery schedule as stated in the proposal. Additional working days may apply depending on the city’s building permit application duration with Client’s notice. Delays in progress billing will result in additional working days.
4. Retention of Title / Clause of Ownership
Ownership of the discussed project shall remain with SolidWurth Corp. in case 100% payment is not settled by the Client. The Client is not allowed to sell the goods to third parties, nor to cede or to mortgage or to give them as a security, either in the original or in altered state, as long as the goods have not been paid in full.
5. Offer Validity
2 weeks from offer date.
6. Change Orders
In the event that Change Orders are received after the permanent structures have been erected into construction, SolidWurth will inform the client about eventual costs that need to be settled and possible consequences on erection time. Reduction in scope of supply in principal is not allowed.
7. Intellectual Property Rights
All drawings and technical documents relating to the Project submitted by one party to the other prior or subsequent to the formation of the Contract shall remain the property of the submitting party. Drawings, technical documents or other technical information received by one party shall not, without the consent of the other party, be used for any other purpose than that for which they were provided. They may not, without the consent of the submitting party, otherwise be used or copied, reproduced, transmitted or communicated to a third party. SolidWurth shall not be obliged to provide construction drawings for the Project.
8. Penalty for Late Payment
In the event that the buyer does not punctually fulfill its payment obligations as specified in this quotation, SolidWurth shall be entitled to claim a penalty for late payment of 0.1% of the outstanding balance per day the payment is delayed. This penalty for late payment does not interfere or replace SolidWurth’s right to claim full damages and losses caused by the late payment.
9. Cancellation of Purchase Order
To cancel an official Purchase Order, please submit written notice duly signed by authorized persons. For cancelled Purchase Orders, client agrees to pay SolidWurth an equivalent amount of 30% of Total Project Cost in addition to cost of produced materials.
10. Warranty
All constructed civil works mentioned above carry a FIVE (5) YEARS warranty except resulting from force majeure and natural failure.
11. Project Take Over Procedure
At the end of the installation, Acceptance Certificate will be signed which is the start of the guarantee. In case no Acceptance Certificate has been signed, the date that the buyer starts using or occupying the project for commercial purposes will be considered as take over date and start of the guarantee period.
12. Construction Utilities
The Client should make the site available for the contractor. The contractor should keep the provided said utilities for sole construction purposes only. Electricity and water supply for construction purposes should be provided by the client.
13. Construction Safety and Health Program
The contractor should comply to DOLE’s safety requirements. The client can intervene with the construction due to safety concerns.
14. No Damage for Delay
The contractor shall not have any claim for compensation for damages against the owner for any stoppage or delay from any cause whatsoever.
15. Applicable Law
Philippine Law is applicable. In case of translation of this document in any language, the English version will be binding.
16. Labor, Tools and Materials
All labor, tools, equipment, and materials to be used in the construction will be handled by the contractor.
